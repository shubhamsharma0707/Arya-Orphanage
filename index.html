ABOUT US

United in compassion, changing lives

Arya Anathalaya (estb. 1918) is one of the oldest and best places for underprivileged and downtrodden children. We provide school and residence facilities, currently supporting around 700 children with care, education, and guidance.

OUR APPROACH

Compassionate solutions for lasting impact

We focus on the holistic empowerment of underprivileged children. By providing education, residence, and guidance, we ensure a bright future for them. Running such an institution requires community support, and we welcome everyone to come forward and contribute.
